About Me

I help organizations and professionals make sense of AI-powered research tools, ensuring insights are accurate, relevant, and actionable for real-world health and policy challenges. As an epidemiologist, public health researcher, and policy analyst, I specialize in the intersection of environmental health, epidemiology, and mental health policy.

  • ✔ Health Equity & Data Science: Applying data-driven approaches to tackle public health challenges and ensure evidence-based policies.
  • ✔ Program Evaluation & Policy Impact: Measuring the effectiveness of programs for long-term success.
  • ✔ Science Communication & Knowledge Translation: Converting complex research into actionable reports, policy briefs, and strategic insights.
